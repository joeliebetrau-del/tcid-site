Submission Template

How the template works.

The structured template is the fastest path to TCID for any dealer with organized inventory data. Fill in the fields, submit, get your file back with TCIDs populated.

01

Download

Download the Excel template. It includes dropdown validation for key fields, 10,000 rows, and three example cards to show the format.

02

Fill In

Enter your inventory one card type per row. Required fields are highlighted blue. Optional fields are white. Delete the example rows before submitting.

03

Submit by Email

Email your completed template to info@tradingcardid.org. We process submissions and return results within one business day.

04

Review Results

Your file comes back with TCID, Match Status, and Confidence for every row. Filter by Match Status -- Exact and High Confidence rows are done. Review Required rows need a second look.
